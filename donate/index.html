Ethiopian Orphans Support-UK Charity Fund (EOS-UK Charity Fund) depends entirely on donations from the public for its income that supports projects at Elshadai-Wukro Children's Village in Wukro. To give you some idea, it costs £20.00 (2016 prices) to maintain a child for a month – food, clothing, education, books, etc.... However you can give whatever you can afford. If you would like to help the fund you can contribute in a number of ways:

  • you can make a one-off donation (click here);
  • you can make a regular donation monthly (the preferred method for certainty of income) by banker’s order, or quarterly, half-yearly or annually. If you prefer regular donation, please click here
